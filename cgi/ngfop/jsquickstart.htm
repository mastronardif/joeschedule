JoeSchedule.com is a Website for creating picture schedules and other visual aids. It allows you to keep all your pictures and schedules on the internet in one convenient place. You can access your picture schedules from anywhere. You have access to over 500 million pictures from google, yahoo, or your own pictures. You can email them to family and friends.

There is a calendar in which you can organize your calendar with pictures.

The most useful feature explained.

We are going to copy a picture URL from google images and paste it in our new schedule named My Schedule.

0. Main Screen

1. Click New on the top of the page. The right displays a blank schedule. Type My Schedule over blank schedule in the edit box on the right side.

2. Click on the camera icon "My Picture". on the top of the page. The screen displays as follows in the middle and right panes:

3. Type School Bus in the edit box in the middle pane.
Press Lazy Boy. Another Browser window should come up with google images of school buses
My Pictures Lazy Boy

Copy Paste the picture URL. as follows:
Copy Picture URL into the copy buffer.
1. Switch to the Google Images Window(Alt Tab in Windows).
2. Move your mouse cursor on to the picture. the cursor should turn into a hand.
3. When over the picture Right mouse click and select properties from the drop down menu.
4. Mark a little area over the text for the address (URL) Hold down the Right mouse button and drag over a couple of the letters on htpp://....
5. Right mouse click and select Select All from the drop down menu.
5. Right mouse click and select Copy from the drop down menu.
Now the Picture URL is in the copy buffer.

Paste Picture URL from the copy buffer.
1. Go back to the Joeschedule window.
2. Move the mouse cursor to the empty edit box in the middle pane.
3. Right mouse click and select Paste from the drop down menu.
4. The picture URL should be in the edit box now. the edit box now contains the text htpp://....

5. Paste picture URL in edit box in the middle

My Pictures Lazy Boy

6. Press Radio button to the left of the edit box. The image should appear in the middle if the picture URL is correct.

6a. Press the Radio button on the row you want to copy the image to on your schedule on the RIGHT.

My Pictures Lazy Boy

7. Click on the copy to the right icon on the top of the page. The picture should move to the right.
