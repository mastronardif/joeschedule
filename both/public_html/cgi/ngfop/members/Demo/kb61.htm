George Washington

Hi, My name is George Washington and I'm the first president of the United States.

I was born on a farm in Virginia, in 1732.

I went to school until I was 15 years old.

A war began with England called the American Revolutionary War.

I was asked to lead the army.

We won the war in 1783.

I was a hero.

In 1789 I was elected America's first president.

I served two terms as President.

Today, people think of me as 'The Father of Our Country'.
Mara, W. (2002). George Washington. New York:Children's Press.
