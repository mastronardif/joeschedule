Make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Get bowl.

Get spoon.

Get one stick of butter.

Get one egg.

Get cookie mix.
Preheat oven temperature to 350 degrees.

Put butter in bowl.

Put bowl in microwave for 60 seconds.
Take bowl out of microwave.

Add cookie mix to bowl.

Add egg to bowl.

Stir.

Put rounded tablespoon of batter onto cookie sheet.

Put on oven mitts.

Put cookie sheets in oven.

Set timer for nine minutes.

Let cool.
Eat them.
